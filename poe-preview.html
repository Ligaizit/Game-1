About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off endless waves of increasingly challenging monsters. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, you'll need to utilize various weapons, abilities, and strategies to survive as long as possible.

With procedurally generated maps, diverse enemy types, and an extensive upgrade system, each playthrough offers a unique experience. Can you unlock all character abilities and become the ultimate monster survivor?

Game Features

  • • Auto-attack combat system with manual movement
  • • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities
  • • Dozens of weapons and passive items to discover
  • • Challenging boss encounters and special events
  • • Level up and evolve your abilities during each run

Gameplay Guide

Controls

Keyboard

WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character

Space to use special ability (when available)

ESC to pause the game

Mobile Controls

Touch joystick in bottom-left corner to move

Tap ability button when it's charged

Menu button to access pause options

How To Play

In Monster Survivors, your character automatically attacks nearby enemies. Your primary goal is to navigate the arena, avoiding enemy attacks while positioning yourself for maximum effectiveness.

As you defeat monsters, you'll gain experience points. Level up to choose between three random upgrades for your weapons or passive items. Strategically select items that complement your character's abilities and playstyle.

Every few minutes, challenging boss monsters will appear. These encounters require careful movement and tactical awareness to survive. The longer you play, the more difficult the game becomes as monster density and strength increase.

Character Progression

Each run helps you collect coins that can be spent in the meta-progression system. Between runs, you can unlock new characters, starting weapons, and permanent upgrades to increase your chances of survival.

Experiment with different character and weapon combinations to discover powerful synergies. Some characters excel at area damage, while others focus on single-target elimination or survivability. Finding your preferred playstyle is key to mastering Monster Survivors.

Survival Tips & Tricks

Beginner Tips

  • • Focus on movement and dodging enemy attacks
  • • Collect experience gems quickly before they disappear
  • • Prioritize health regeneration items early on
  • • Try to evolve at least one weapon to its maximum level

Advanced Strategies

  • • Kite enemies in circular patterns to group them together
  • • Balance offensive and defensive item selections
  • • Save power-ups for boss encounters when possible
  • • Learn enemy attack patterns to predict their movements

Secret Techniques

  • • Destroy environmental objects for hidden rewards
  • • Certain weapon combinations unlock special synergy effects
  • • Each map has secret areas with valuable resources
  • • Time your level-ups strategically for instant health recovery
