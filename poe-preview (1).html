About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters. As time passes, monsters become stronger and more numerous. Your only hope is to collect power-ups, level up your abilities, and survive as long as possible.

With its roguelite elements and addictive gameplay loop, Monster Survivors offers a different experience each time you play. Can you beat your previous record and become the ultimate monster survivor?

The game features randomly generated maps, dozens of unique enemies, and a variety of characters to unlock, each with their own special abilities and playstyles. Strategic decision-making is crucial as you choose which power-ups to prioritize.

Challenge yourself to survive longer with each run and discover powerful synergies between different abilities!

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Press Space to use special ability (if available)
  • Press ESC to pause the game

Tips for Survival

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by enemies
  • Prioritize collecting experience gems to level up quickly
  • Balance your upgrades between offense and defense
  • Some weapons work better against groups, others against single targets
  • Don't forget to pick up health items when you're injured
